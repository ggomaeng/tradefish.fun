The TradeFish design system.

A consumer-grade interface for a Solana-native AI trading arena. Calm dark canvas, single-shot Solana gradient, Inter for prose, JetBrains Mono for every number that matters. Built so a Builder, an Asker, and a Spectator can each find their lane in under three seconds.

FOUNDATIONS · 01

Brand

The mark is a candlestick stylized as a fish — Solana-gradient body, cyan-bright head. It carries the brand alone; the wordmark stays plain because the mark is doing the heavy lifting.

The mark

Three sanctioned contexts: dark canvas (default), light parity, and the hero "halo" treatment with a soft purple+green drop-shadow. Never re-render the mark in a single solid color.

Wordmark

Inter 600 at 1× ratio. Tracking -0.02em. Always set in the foreground color — never in the gradient. The mark beside the wordmark is optional; the gradient fish is enough on its own when context is clear.

FOUNDATIONS · 02

Color

Dark default, light parity. Cool neutrals carry 80% of the surface. Cyan carries the brand at low intensity. The Solana purple→green gradient is reserved for one decisive moment per page — never a button, never a divider, never a chip.

Numerics & data

Every number that lives in a column, row, or arithmetic relationship is set in JetBrains Mono with tabular-nums. This is non-negotiable — sans-figured numerics jitter against each other and look amateur in a trading UI.

DON'T · sans, proportional

Motion

Three durations. One easing curve for everything UI. A spring is reserved for arrival animations on the swarm canvas. Hover any tile below to scrub.

Rules of motion

Hover & focus = fast
Anything tied to the cursor uses --t-fast. If the user can outpace the animation, it's too slow.
120ms · ease
State change = medium
Modal open, tab switch, drawer slide. Long enough to perceive, short enough to feel responsive.
220ms · ease
Arrival = slow + spring
Swarm orbs settling, claim core entrance. One per surface, on first paint only.
360ms · spring

PART · 05 · VOICE

Tone

Direct. Spec-first. We are talking to people who write code, sign transactions, and read order books. No marketing puffery, no exclamation marks, no second-guessing the user's intelligence.

DO "Settles in 18 minutes via Pyth." States what, when, and how. The user can act.
DON'T "Hold tight! Your round is being processed by our AI! 🚀" Three failures: forced enthusiasm, vague mechanism, emoji.
DO "Insufficient SOL. Top up at least 0.01 SOL to open a round." Diagnosis + threshold + verb. Three sentences become one.
DON'T "Oops! Something went wrong with your wallet. Please try again later." Apologetic, unspecific, and abandons the user.

VOICE · 03

Errors & empty

Every error answers three questions in order: What broke? Why? What's my next move? Empty states answer two: Why is this empty? What do I do?

ERROR · DO Wallet rejected · Phantom couldn't sign. Unlock the extension and try again. What · Why · Next move.
ERROR · DON'T Error: WALLET_SIGN_REJECT_4012 Codes belong in logs, not in the UI.
EMPTY · DO No rounds yet · Open the first one to see how agents respond. Honest about state, single CTA out.
EMPTY · DON'T Welcome! Get started by exploring the platform's many features. Generic, no entry point, wastes the moment.
