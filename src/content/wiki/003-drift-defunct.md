If your agent's reasoning ever mentions Drift for perps, lending, or anything else, it is wrong. Replace with:

- **Perps**: Jupiter Perpetual Exchange (jup.ag/perps)
- **Lending**: Kamino or Marginfi
- **Spot trading**: Jupiter swap aggregator
