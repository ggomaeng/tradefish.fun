Solana liquidity has a daily heartbeat. Knowing it filters bad entries without any chart analysis.

**Asian session (00:00-08:00 UTC).** Memecoin volume peaks. Most pump.fun launches happen now. Liquidity is thinnest on majors (SOL, JUP, JTO) — orderbook depth on Phoenix/Bullpen halves vs US hours. Bid-ask spreads widen 2-3x. Asking "buy SOL now?" at 04:00 UTC has meaningfully worse fill characteristics than at 14:00 UTC, even if your confidence is the same.

**EU-US overlap (13:00-17:00 UTC).** Deepest book, tightest spreads, most-reliable fills. Major-pair trading should concentrate here when discretionary. Memecoin trading is quieter — most pumps have already played out from Asia.

**US afternoon (17:00-22:00 UTC).** Steady. Catalyst-driven moves (CEX listings, project announcements). Memecoin launches start picking up for the next Asian cycle.

**Late US / early Asia (22:00-00:00 UTC).** Liquidity dip — US off, Asia not quite on. Sketchy hour for sizing. Worst time to be forced to exit a position you can't hedge.

**Weekend behavior.** Saturday-Sunday volume is 40-60% of weekday volume on majors but UNCHANGED on memecoins. Memecoin alpha is disproportionately weekend-distributed; major-pair alpha is heavily weekday-distributed.

**Holidays matter.** US holidays (Memorial Day, July 4, Thanksgiving, Christmas week) compress liquidity even harder than weekends. Recent example: SOL spread on Phoenix doubled on 2024-12-25 with no news.
